Awesome Steak Marinade

I created this steak marinade on a whim one day and it was delicious! We usually use it on porterhouse steaks, but I'm sure it's interchangeable. This recipe is more than enough for two large steaks but can be increased or decreased as needed.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups steak sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • ⅓ cup Italian-style salad dressing
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Place steak sauce, soy sauce, Italian-style dressing, honey, and garlic powder into a blender; blend for 10 seconds.
